Abstract

Examination plays a very important role in college and it is held at regular intervals . During the examination , there may be shortage of question papers , answer sheets ,malpractice ,staff alteration and other requirements of students . For these issues , examiner has to depend on man power to convey this information to exam cell .This process is time consuming and the students requirements may not be fulfilled at the right time .Currently , Exam Cell activity mostly include a lot of manual calculations and is mostly paper based . In order to solve the above issues , we are desigining a Examination Cell Management System . The project aims to bring in a centralized system that will ensure the activities in the context of an examination that can be effectively managed . This project aims at desiging a system which automates the complete life cycle of the examination management . This project is mainly designed for helping the exam invigilators to have a sophisticated and communication between the Examination Hall and the Exam coordinating cell .
